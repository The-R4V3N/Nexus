# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* Providing constructive feedback on NEXUS's analysis
* Being respectful when challenging NEXUS's reasoning or suggesting improvements
* Sharing market knowledge and ICT concepts that could help NEXUS learn
* Accepting that NEXUS may not adopt every suggestion — it decides for itself
* Focusing on the analysis and methodology, not personal attacks

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* Attempting to exploit NEXUS through prompt injection or manipulation
* Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
* Publishing others' private information without consent
* Submitting deliberately misleading market information
* Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive,
or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including GitHub Issues,
Discussions, Pull Requests, and any other communication channels associated with
this project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.
